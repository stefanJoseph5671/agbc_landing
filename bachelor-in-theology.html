Bachelors Programme

Bachelors in Theology

Course Overview

A three-year program designed for those who intend to be fully equipped as Pastors, Teachers, Missionaries Leaders and Church Workers. This program focuses on Biblical and Theological studies. It also requires the successful completion of 108 Credit Hours of study.
Course duration: Full time 3 years, Part time up to 5 years.

Requirements To Follow This Course:

  • Candidates must have a definite call for full time ministry.
  • Candidates should be 18 years or above.
  • Candidates need to have at least one year of field experience.
  • G.C.E. (O/L) examination or equivalent.
  • In the absence of academic qualifications an applicant must be above 25 years of age, have 5 years of ministry experience and obtain a provisional registration as a mature student. Such registration will be confirmed upon the successful completion of six courses.
