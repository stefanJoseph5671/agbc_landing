About Our Philosophy

A premier ministerial training and theological institution.

AGBC provides a spirit-filled, academic environment for students to acquire knowledge, scholarship, attitudes, abilities, desire and spiritual character that will enable them to serve as Pastors, Teachers, Evangelists, Missionaries, Prophets or fruitful members of the church, in Sri Lanka and to “the ends of the earth”.

  • Training Leaders

    We disciple and train leaders to commission them for the fivefold ministry of the church.

  • Equipping Pastors and Leaders

    We equip pastors and leaders to be Spirit-filled channels of service with the best possible tools.

  • Transforming Communities

    We let God transform the student to transform our communities and whichever situation God places them in.
